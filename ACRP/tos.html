ACRP Rules

Illegal Activity:

We strictly prohibit engaging in illegal activities on the ACRP platform. This includes searching, asking about, or sharing illegal content. Accounts found in violation will be terminated, and relevant billing information will be forwarded to the appropriate authorities for further action.

Scamming:

Scamming is not tolerated on the ACRP platform. Posting malicious links that lead to scam websites is also prohibited. IP addresses will be traced, and necessary actions will be taken.

Harassment/Blackmailing/Threatening:

We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, blackmail, and threats towards other users on ACRP. IP addresses will be tracked and reported to the appropriate authorities.

Repeated Violation:

Repeated violations are not acceptable on ACRP. After three chances, users who fail to adhere to the rules will face permanent account suspension.

Sexual Content:

Any form of explicit sexual content is strictly prohibited on the ACRP platform. Violators will have their accounts permanently banned.

Child Pornography (CP):

The depiction or sharing of child pornography is a severe violation on ACRP. IP addresses will be tracked and reported to the authorities for legal action.

Cheating (3rd Offense):

The use of mod menus or any form of cheating is against the rules on ACRP. Accounts will be suspended for 190 days upon a third offense.

Bribery of a Mod:

Attempting to bribe a moderator is a grave offense on ACRP. Accounts will be suspended for 190 days as a consequence.

Cheating (2nd Offense):

Cheating undermines the integrity of the ACRP platform. Accounts will be suspended for 7 days upon a second offense, along with evidence against the user.

FailRP (3rd Offense):

Failure to roleplay properly disrupts the experience for others on ACRP. A 7-day ban will be issued upon a third offense. Continued offenses may result in permanent suspension.

Random Deathmatch (RDM):

Engaging in random deathmatch is not allowed on ACRP. A 7-day ban will be issued upon an offense. Repeating such actions may lead to a permanent ban.

Cheating (1st Offense):

The first offense related to cheating will result in a 1-day ban on ACRP. We do not tolerate cheating on our platform.

FailRP (2nd Offense):

A 1-day ban will be issued for a second offense of failure to roleplay correctly on ACRP.

FailRP (1st Offense):

This is the first warning for failure to roleplay properly on ACRP. Users are advised to correct their behavior, as further offenses may lead to a ban.
