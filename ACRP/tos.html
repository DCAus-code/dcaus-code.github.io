ACRP - Server Rules

1. Respect and Behavior

  1. Respect all players, staff members, and their opinions. Use appropriate language and avoid offensive remarks.
  2. Harassment, bullying, and trolling are strictly prohibited. Treat others with courtesy and kindness.
  3. Do not engage in discriminatory behavior based on race, gender, age, or any other factors.
  4. Maintain a positive and welcoming atmosphere for everyone.

2. Roleplay Guidelines

  1. Stay in-character during roleplay scenarios. Do not break character without valid roleplay reasons.
  2. Follow realistic behavior and actions that align with your character's role and background.
  3. Do not engage in god-modding or overpowered actions that disrupt roleplay.
  4. Engage in meaningful roleplay interactions that enhance the experience for all participants.

3. Metagaming and Powergaming

  1. Do not use out-of-character information in roleplay (metagaming).
  2. Avoid forcing actions on other players without providing them a fair chance to respond (powergaming).
  3. Realistic and immersive roleplay takes precedence over game mechanics.

4. Chat and Communication

  1. Use appropriate in-game chat channels for different types of communication.
  2. Do not spam, flood, or excessively use chat channels.
  3. Keep your messages roleplay-focused and relevant to the ongoing scenario.

5. Vehicle and Property Usage

  1. Obey traffic rules and drive realistically within the server.
  2. Do not steal, damage, or misuse other players' vehicles or properties without proper roleplay justification.
  3. Engage in proper property roleplay, such as renting or purchasing properties through roleplay transactions.

6. Emergency Services (EMS)

  1. If your character dies during a roleplay scenario, wait for EMS to arrive and treat you.
  2. If EMS is not online or does not respond within a reasonable time, use common sense to proceed with roleplay.

7. Staff Interaction

  1. Follow staff instructions and decisions. They are here to ensure fair and enjoyable gameplay.
  2. Do not argue or disrespect staff members. Address concerns through proper communication channels.

8. Reporting and Appeals

  1. Report rule violations to staff with evidence. Do not take matters into your own hands.
  2. If you believe you were wrongly punished, use the appropriate appeals process to address the issue.

9. Character Creation and Background

  1. Create a realistic and detailed character background that fits within the server's setting.
  2. Respect other players' character backgrounds and contribute to immersive roleplay interactions.

10. Content Creation and Sharing

  1. Follow the server's content creation guidelines when sharing screenshots, videos, or other media.
  2. Ensure that shared content aligns with the server's themes and atmosphere.

11. No Random Deathmatching (RDM)

  1. Do not engage in random attacks or killing of other players without valid roleplay reasons.
  2. All combat and hostile actions must be properly roleplayed and justified.

12. No Vehicle Deathmatching (VDM)

  1. Do not use vehicles to randomly harm, kill, or disrupt other players' roleplay experiences.
  2. Follow realistic vehicle interactions and collisions during roleplay scenarios.

13. No Fail Roleplay (FRP)

  1. Avoid engaging in actions that break immersion or realism within the roleplay setting.
  2. Do not misuse roleplay scenarios for unrealistic gains or actions.

14. Roleplay involving Alcohol and Drugs

  1. You may roleplay scenarios involving drunk driving or being under the influence of alcohol, but do not depict being high on drugs.
  2. Drug-related roleplay is prohibited, with the exception of roleplaying scenarios involving drugged brownies.
  3. Respect the server's rating and guidelines when portraying sensitive topics.

Penalty Point System

Rule violations will result in penalty points based on severity:

Violation Severity Penalty Points Penalty Duration

Moderate Infractions 10 Points 7-Day Ban
Severe Infractions 15 Points 1-Month Ban
Extreme Infractions 20 Points 190-Day or 6-Month Ban (Depending on Severity)
