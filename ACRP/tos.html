Respect and Etiquette: Treat all members with respect and kindness. Avoid offensive language, harassment, and discrimination. Personal attacks will not be tolerated.

No NSFW Content

Keep all content safe for work (SFW). This includes text, images, links, and discussions. Any explicit or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to ACRP and roleplay-related topics. Avoid spamming or derailing conversations.

Use Appropriate Channels

Post content in the appropriate channels. Check channel descriptions to ensure you're sharing content in the right place.

No Advertising

Avoid self-promotion, advertisements, or recruitment for other communities. Exceptions may be made with staff approval.

Respect Staff Decisions

Follow the instructions of staff members. Disobeying staff, arguing, or disrespecting their decisions will result in consequences.

No Drama or Disputes

Do not engage in public drama, arguments, or disputes. If you have an issue, address it privately with the involved parties or staff.

No Mic or Text Spam

Avoid excessive use of voice chat or text messages that flood the chat. Respect others' space to communicate.

No Personal Information

Do not share personal information about yourself or others. Protect your privacy and that of others.

No Unauthorized Bots

Do not add or use bots without staff approval. Unauthorized bots will be removed.

No Impersonation

Do not impersonate staff members, roleplay characters, or other members. Be yourself!

No Trolling or Harassment

Do not troll, harass, or intentionally provoke other members. Keep discussions civil and respectful.

Use English Language

Communicate primarily in English to ensure inclusivity and understanding among members.

Roleplay Etiquette

If using roleplay channels, follow roleplay etiquette and stay in character during roleplay discussions.

No Backseat Moderating

Leave moderation to the staff. If you notice a rule violation, report it to staff instead of intervening.

Failure to comply with these rules may result in warnings, mute, kick, or ban, depending on the severity of the violation and at the staff's discretion.

By being part of the ACRP Discord server, you agree to adhere to these rules. Enjoy your time here and contribute positively to our community!
