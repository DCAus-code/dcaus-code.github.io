ContentLTD Privacy Policy


Effective Date: 11/23/24

Last Updated: 11/23/24


This Privacy Policy explains how ContentLTD (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our Services, including Emergency Response: Liberty County (ER:LC) Roblox servers, associated Discord servers, and official websites (collectively, the “Services”). By using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you must not use our Services.

1. Information We Collect


We may collect the following types of information:


  • Personal Information: Name, username, email address, IP address, and account details on Roblox, Discord, or linked services like Bloxlink.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent, edited, and deleted in our Discord servers, and support tickets submitted within the servers.
  • Usage Data: Gameplay statistics, connection logs, and activity patterns on our Roblox servers and websites.
  • Technical Data: Device type, browser type, operating system, and other technical identifiers.
  • Geolocation Data: Country or region based on IP address for compliance and localization purposes.



2. How We Use Your Information


We use your information to:


  • Provide and maintain our Services, including account verification and gameplay functionality.
  • Monitor and moderate activity within our Discord servers and Roblox servers to ensure rule compliance.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and respond to violations.
  • Analyze usage data to improve our Services and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations, including cooperating with law enforcement.



3. Information Sharing and Disclosure


We may share your information under the following circumstances:


  • With Law Enforcement: To comply with legal obligations or in response to lawful requests.
  • With Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with hosting, moderation, analytics, or technical support.
  • With Third-Party Services: Integrated services such as Roblox, Discord, and Bloxlink, governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • For Legal Protection: To protect our rights, users, or Services in case of disputes or security concerns.



4. Legal Bases for Processing


Depending on your location, we process your personal information under the following legal bases:


  • GDPR (EEA): Based on your consent, to fulfill contractual obligations, or to comply with legal obligations.
  • CCPA (California): As necessary to provide Services or with your consent for data collection and use.
  • LGPD (Brazil): Based on legitimate interest, consent, or compliance with legal obligations.
  • PIPEDA (Canada): With your consent or as required for the provision of Services.
  • Other Jurisdictions: As required or permitted by local data protection laws.



5. Your Rights


Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:


  • Access: Request access to personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal limitations.
  • Data Portability: Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Restriction: Request limitations on how we process your information.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of targeted advertising or data selling (where applicable).



6. Data Protection and Security


We employ robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.




7. International Users


We comply with privacy laws applicable in different jurisdictions, including:


  • GDPR: Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) have enhanced rights, including the right to withdraw consent at any time.
  • CCPA: California residents can request disclosure of how their information is collected, used, or shared and can opt out of data selling.
  • LGPD: Brazilian users can request confirmation of data processing, access to data, or portability of data.
  • Other Countries: We respect local data protection laws where our users reside.



8. Data Retention


We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our Terms of Service. Logged communications in Discord and gameplay data in Roblox may be retained indefinitely unless a deletion request is approved under applicable laws.




9. Children’s Privacy


We require users to meet the minimum age for social media use in their jurisdiction to access our Services. For users under the age of majority, parental consent is required. For more information, refer to our Age Requirements section in the Terms of Service.




10. Changes to This Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated “Last Updated” date. Continued use of our Services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.




11. Contact Us


If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:


Email: info@contentltd.net
