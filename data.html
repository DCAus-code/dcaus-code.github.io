Data Processing at Sageimatic

1. Shipment Information

1.1 Email and Phone Number

When you provide us with your email and phone number for shipments, we use this information exclusively for efficient communication related to your orders. This includes updates on order status, delivery notifications, and any pertinent information regarding your shipment.

1.2 Shipment Partners

To facilitate smooth deliveries, we collaborate with trusted shipping companies like FedEx. Your provided email and phone number are shared with these shipping partners solely for the purpose of delivering your orders. Our partners adhere to strict confidentiality agreements, ensuring responsible handling of your information.

1.3 Subscription for Updates

By sharing your email and phone number for shipments, you automatically subscribe to receive updates about your delivery. These updates are designed to keep you informed about the progress of your order and any relevant details.

2. General Communication

2.1 Important Notices

We may use the contact information you provide to communicate essential details related to your account, invoices, and other critical matters. This ensures that you stay informed about important developments that require your attention.

2.2 No Selling of Data

Sageimatic is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is treated with the utmost confidentiality and is used only for the specific purposes outlined in this policy.

3. Security Measures

To safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction, Sageimatic employs industry-standard security measures. We continuously update our practices to align with the latest standards and technologies, prioritizing the security of your information.

4. Consent and Control

By providing your information, you consent to the practices described in this "How We Process Data" page. We understand the importance of giving you control over your personal data. Sageimatic provides options to update, correct, or delete your information as needed.

If you have any questions or concerns about our data processing practices, please feel free to contact us at sageimatic@gmail.com. Your privacy is a priority for us, and we appreciate your trust in Sageimatic.
