NOV 8, 2024

As we know, the election for Sherrif and Office of the County Mayor is this december 16 - 20th. So here is some advice so you can vote with confidence!

Dont try to vote twice -- You may be banned from voting

We track votes by IP, we will NOT store or sell your IP. This is to simply prevent spam and double voting.

Dont offer to pay or accept deals to get paid to vote for a candidate.

Feel free to make predictions, make it clear its a prediction

And finally take your time.

Keeping those rules in mind, you will be successful! Remember, if your selected candidate does not win just know you tried.

We will take from december 23rd to the 27th to count votes. Dont harass us for results. We are working tirelessly.

Remember to vote!
